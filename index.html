20 Historical Places in India
Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal was commissioned by Shah Jahan in 1631, built in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The Qutb Minar was constructed by Qutub-ud-Din Aibak and later completed by Iltutmish.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal is a palace in Jaipur, Rajasthan, built from red and pink sandstone.

Amber Fort

Amber was a Meena state before being captured by Kachwahas and turned into a fort.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a royal residence in Mysore, Karnataka.
